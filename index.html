Industrial geometry

Heavy cuff bracelets inspired by the aesthetics of mechanical design and brutalism. The massive hinged structure combined with the matte, artificially aged surface conveys a feeling of cold steel power.

Embossed rivets with symbolic engraving and internal patterns set a strict rhythm, balancing on the edge between rough utilitarianism and jewelry detailing.

Gothic monumentalism

A massive pendant that reinterprets the classic dark aesthetic through a complex, aggressive geometry. The multilevel architecture of the product with an abundance of sculptural details — from the central figure to the skull at the base — creates a deep play of light and shadow.

Patinated metal and chopped, towering lines emphasize the brutal nature of the jewelry, turning it into an expressive modern artifact.
